New York Times columnist and author,Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as "the most famous financial journalistof his generation." His book, Too Big to Fail, was the first behind-the-scenes account of financial crisis that let to our current recession. The Economist, The Financial Times, and Business Week all named Too Big To Fail one of the best books of the year. The books was published by Viking October 20. 2009.
